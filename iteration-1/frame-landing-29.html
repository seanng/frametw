About Frame

Grid-First Philosophy

At Frame, we believe great design starts with structure. Our grid-based approach ensures every element has purpose and place, creating harmony between form and function.

We're not just designers and developers – we're digital architects. Every project begins with a systematic analysis of needs, goals, and possibilities. From there, we build experiences that are as beautiful as they are functional.

Our methodology combines Swiss design principles with modern web technologies, resulting in digital products that stand the test of time. Clean lines, clear hierarchy, and purposeful interactions define our work.
